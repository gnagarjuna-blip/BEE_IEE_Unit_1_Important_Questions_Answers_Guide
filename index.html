1. State and explain KCL and KVL 5M / 10M

Kirchhoff's Current Law (KCL)

Statement (2M): The algebraic sum of currents meeting at a junction (node) in an electrical circuit is zero. Sum of incoming currents = Sum of outgoing currents.

[Image of Kirchhoff's Current Law diagram showing currents entering and leaving a node]

Explanation: KCL is based on the Law of Conservation of Charge. It means no charge can accumulate at a node.

Kirchhoff's Voltage Law (KVL)

Statement (2M): In any closed loop of a network, the algebraic sum of all voltages (EMFs and IR drops) is zero.

Explanation: KVL is based on the Law of Conservation of Energy. The energy supplied by sources must equal energy consumed by loads.

4. Series R-C Circuit Numerical (R=10, C=0.1, V=20) 10M

Problem: Determine current equation and voltages at t=0.

The general current equation for charging RC is: i(t) = (V/R) * exp(-t / RC).
Given: V = 20, R = 10, C = 0.1.
Time Constant (RC) = 10 * 0.1 = 1 second.
i(t) = 2 * exp(-t) Amperes.
At t = 0, exp(0) = 1.
Current i(0) = 2 Amperes.
Voltage across Resistor (Vr) = i(0) * R = 2 * 10 = 20V.
Voltage across Capacitor (Vc) = V - Vr = 20 - 20 = 0V.
8(I). Nodal Analysis (Find V1 and V2) 10M

Based on the green circuit with 1A and 4A current sources:

Node 1: 1 = V1/2 + (V1 - V2)/6
Multiply by 6: 6 = 3V1 + V1 - V2 => 4V1 - V2 = 6 --- (1)

Node 2: (V1 - V2)/6 = V2/7 + 4
Multiply by 42: 7V1 - 7V2 = 6V2 + 168 => 7V1 - 13V2 = 168 --- (2)
Solving (1) and (2):
V1 = (13*6 - 168) / (13*4 - 7) = (78 - 168) / 45 = -2V.
V2 = 4V1 - 6 = 4(-2) - 6 = -14V.
Result: V1 = -2V, V2 = -14V.
8(II). Mesh Analysis (Current through 10 Ohm) 10M

Based on the circuit with 50V and 25V sources:

Mesh 1: 50 - 10*I1 - 5(I1 - I2) = 0 => 15*I1 - 5*I2 = 50.
Mesh 2: -5(I2 - I1) - 10*I2 - 25 = 0 => -5*I1 + 15*I2 = -25.
Solving for I1: 15*I1 - 5*I2 = 50, and -15*I1 + 45*I2 = -75.
40*I2 = -25 => I2 = -0.625A.
I1 = 3.125A.
Current through left 10 Ohm = 3.125A.
10. Series Resistance Problem (2, 4, 6 Ohms) 5M / 10M

Total resistance (Rt) = 2 + 4 + 6 = 12 Ohms.
Total Current (I) = V / Rt = 24 / 12 = 2A.

Voltages:
V across 2 Ohm = 2 * 2 = 4V.
V across 4 Ohm = 2 * 4 = 8V.
V across 6 Ohm = 2 * 6 = 12V.
Total Power = V * I = 24 * 2 = 48W.
